Welcome to the Pigeon Tracking App
A few years ago I saw one-legged pigeons all around the city. But when I told my girlfriend about them, she said she'd never seen a single one. Since then she has been pointing out every two-legged pigeon we've come across. So I created this app to be able to track all the pigeons we see, one-legged or two-legged. Hopefully we'll clear up our dispute and I'll be able to prove these one-legged pigeons are still around!
Have fun adding pigeons to the map!
